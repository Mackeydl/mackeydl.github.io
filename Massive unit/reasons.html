Reasons for
obtaining Power

In Macbeth, there are many reasons for obtaining power from the divine ability and power that a king can have over his people, to the extreme control over someone's life and their death. Power is specifically sought throughout the play through characters such as Macbeth, Lady Macbeth, Duncan and the witches
